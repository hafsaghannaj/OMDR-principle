# ODMR Principle Visualized

A comprehensive visual guide to **Optically Detected Magnetic Resonance** in Nitrogen-Vacancy centers in diamond.

## What is ODMR?

ODMR is a technique that uses optical methods to detect magnetic resonance transitions in spin systems. In NV centers, it enables room-temperature quantum sensing of magnetic fields, temperature, electric fields, and strain with nanoscale spatial resolution.
